07-storey Residential Building

| Architecture | Educational | Project | Rural |
| 22 june, 2023 | | Total Views: 340 |

Residential Building © CODEC

Name: 07-storey Residential Building
Year: 2021
Location: Bashundhara R/A, Dhaka
Design team: Ar. MD Nabil Askar Upal & Ar. Shariful Ahasan

This project is located in Bashundhara residential area, Dhaka. This seven-storey building is featuring some well-balanced architectural elements. The design was gracefully executed to ensure comfort and optimize living environment of the inhabitants. The bedrooms and formal living cum dining is placed in such a way that it prioritizes the climatic needs required in a residential building.

The extended balconies adjunct to the bedrooms at north and south side, not only creates a peaceful environment, but also works as an architectural element to provide adequate shades to the other lower levels.

| Notes from the Paramount Concord|

The exterior vertical elements were logically placed, enhancing the vertical articulation of the building while drawing a clean and logical thermal boundary around the interiors.

Materials are mainly simple and affordable surfaces, due to the economic possibilities of the context. Exposed brick facades and concrete columns creates a pleasant and exquisite out-look to the viewers.

Another green and sustainable addition to the building was the green balconies at each level to be enjoyed by the residents and visually appealing from the exterior as well.

nd finally, the pergolas at the roof top along with a shaded space not only provides a breathing space to use but also brings out a complete framed out look of the building as well.
